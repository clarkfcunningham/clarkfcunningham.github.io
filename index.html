About Me

I am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree of B.Eng.Biosci through McMaster University's Chemical Engineering and Bioengineering program. This unique program combines all of the core curriculum of McMaster's CEAB accredited Chemical Engineering program with courses in Life Sciences, Health Sciences, and specialized courses of Bioreactors and Bioseparations. In this program, I aim to learn about integration of Biotechnologies with current Chemical Industries, and how new advances in Biosciences can be translated into innovative solutions to problems of Healthcare, Energy, and Sustainable Manufacturing Processes.
